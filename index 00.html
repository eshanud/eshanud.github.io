About

Turning complexity into clarity.

I'm Eshanki UD — a product & packaging designer based in Pune, India. I shape products that feel considered and tactile, from premium packaging and brand systems to clean digital interfaces.

My work sits at the intersection of clarity and care — I sweat the details that make a product feel resolved, whether it's the unboxing moment or the last pixel.

B.Des in Product Design, MIT School of Design (2024). Recent work: Adaara Couture, Drystech, Bio Resources, Electromotion.
